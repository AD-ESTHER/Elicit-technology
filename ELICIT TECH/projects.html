Elicit Technology Infrastructure Limited completed the design and installation of a 1 megawatt solar hybrid energy system at the Ministry of Power's headquarters, located at the Power House Complex. The project featured a triple-panel configuration with integrated hybrid grid battery systems, built for energy flexibility and sustainability.

Our responsibilities included full system commissioning, alignment with grid supply, and the integration of a custom-built Energy Management System (EMS) for real-time monitoring of consumption, system performance, and voltage regulation. This ensured energy optimization, reduced costs, and operational transparency for the client.

The project stands as a testament to Elicit's capabilities in renewable infrastructure and intelligent energy solutions for government institutions.
